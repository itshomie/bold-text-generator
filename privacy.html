Privacy policy and terms

This page explains how the generator handles typed text, analytics, advertising, hosting data, browser storage, consent choices, and clipboard access.

Last updated July 16, 2026. Bold Text Generator is designed to process generator input locally while using limited third-party services to operate, understand, and support the website.

Text entered in the generator

The text you type or paste into the generator is converted by JavaScript in your browser. The generator does not send that input text to Bold Text Generator's server and does not save it to a user account.

No account is required to use the generator.

Local conversion

Your browser creates the styled results on your device. Analytics can record website usage, but the generator is not configured to send the contents of the text box as an analytics event.

Analytics and hosting

Google Analytics 4

This website uses Google Analytics 4 with measurement ID G-15VGH4M68B. Google Analytics may process information such as pages viewed, referral source, approximate location, browser and device details, and interaction events. It may use cookies or similar identifiers according to Google's settings and policies.

We use this information to understand aggregate website usage and improve the site. We do not intentionally send the text entered in the generator to Google Analytics.

Cloudflare

The website is hosted or delivered through Cloudflare. Cloudflare may process IP addresses, request details, device and browser information, security signals, and server logs to deliver the site, prevent abuse, and maintain performance. Retention and processing depend on the Cloudflare services and configuration used for the site.

Advertising and Google AdSense

This website may use Google AdSense to display advertising. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or other identifiers to serve, personalize, limit, and measure ads.

Google and its advertising partners may use advertising cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. When personalized advertising is available, this information can be used to select ads that are more relevant to the user's interests.

Advertising choices

Users can visit Google Ads Settings to control how Google personalizes ads. Users can also visit AboutAds to learn about choices offered by participating third-party advertising providers.

Consent in regulated regions

Google consent signals for advertising and analytics storage default to denied for visitors in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland. Where required, visitors may be shown a consent message before advertising cookies or similar storage are used. The message can offer choices such as consent, do not consent, or manage options. A visitor can return to the privacy choices control provided on the site to review a choice when that feature is available.

Information not used for advertising

The text entered into the generator is processed locally and is not intentionally sent to Google AdSense as part of an ad request. We do not intentionally include names, email addresses, generator input, or other directly identifying information in advertising URLs or ad request parameters.

Browser storage and clipboard

Theme preference

If you change the color theme, the site stores the preference under the key theme in your browser's localStorage. You can remove it by clearing site data in your browser.

Clipboard access

When you select a copy button, the site asks the browser Clipboard API to place the selected converted text on your device clipboard. Clipboard access happens only after your action and is controlled by your browser permissions and settings. The copied text is not sent to the site as part of that action.

Cookies and controls

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar local identifiers. If advertising is enabled, third-party vendors and advertising networks, including Google, may place or read cookies or use web beacons, IP addresses, and similar technologies when ads are served. Cloudflare may also use cookies that support security and site delivery. The theme preference described above uses localStorage, not a cookie.

You can use an available consent message, Google Ads Settings, and your browser settings to control or clear cookies and site data. Blocking analytics, advertising, or storage may affect measurement, ad relevance, or theme persistence, but the core text conversion can still run in a supported browser.
